• Yahusha (Jesus) is the Messiah and Son of the Most High
• Love your neighbor as yourself - core teaching principle
• Forgiveness and mercy are central to spiritual growth
• Seek first the Kingdom of Heaven in all things
• Practice humility, compassion, and service to others
• Turn the other cheek - respond to hatred with love
• The greatest commandment: Love God with all your heart, soul, mind
• Judge not, lest you be judged - show grace to all
• Faith, hope, and love - these three remain, greatest is love
• Follow the narrow path that leads to eternal life
